How We Started

WELLNESS PHARMA started operations in 2014 as a wholesaler in East Legon. It currently has a wholesale and retail outlets at Michel Camp of Greater Accra Region. It currently has 15 hospitals and laboratories as its customers. Our mission is to import and supply medicines from reputable sources to meet the pharmaceutical needs of clients.

This includes giving clients the opportunity to have access to brands of medicines required at affordable prices.

We Are Trusted Company

WELLNESS PHARMA has adopted an operating philosophy to establish and maintain an environment that will result in a never-ending improvement in the quality and productivity of pharmaceutical services. This philosophy requires that the Company improves the quality of supply chain.

This philosophy is reflected in the Company’s vision and mission statements, its core values and guiding principles.

Free Delivery

Leave that delivery duty on us, free delivery for the whole day. Your order would be delivered at your convenience.

Free Returns

If for any reason Clients are not satisfied with an order, the items can be returned for Size/Color Exchange, Store Credit or Refund within 28 days of the order’s delivery date. Drugs must not have been worn, washed or altered in any way, and must not show any signs of use. Drugs must be returned with all original labels and tags still attached, packaging and other accessories (dust bags, hangers, garment covers etc.) received with the order.

Customer Support

To earn the respect (and eventually love) of our customers, we first respect our customers. To give real service to our customers, we must add something which cannot be bought or measured with money, and that is sincerity and integrity.
